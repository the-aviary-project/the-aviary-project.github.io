Meet The Aviary Project

Founded in London, UK, in 2018, we realised that there is no easy way for individuals to meet, share knowledge and access resources in order to build novel projects in the Urban Air Mobility industry. Bringing together our expertise in infrastructure, electric aircraft and entrepreneurship, we decided to change the status quo led by private and closed initiatives.

OUR VISION FOR UAM

We believe in a world where aerial mobility is available to everyone but recognize that without open innovation and the democratization of information, true progress will be hindered.

At The Aviary Project, we are connecting individuals, teams and technologies to support the dawn of an entirely new industry and make the future of transport a reality today!

WHAT DO WE DO?

We aim to enable and support the future of aerial mobility by bringing together the greatest minds in the world and create the defining ecosystem. Through an open-interaction platform, recurring meetups, facilitating industry & startup networks, and summits, we will be bringing together profit and non-profit initiatives in the global stage, whilst giving the opportunity to early-stage and geographically-distant projects to contribute to aerial mobility.

The community

We are a growing community of engineers, entrepreneurs, designers, innovators, policy-makers and stakeholders sharing the same vision for air mobility - join us!

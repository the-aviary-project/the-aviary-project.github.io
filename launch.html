BRINGING AERIAL MOBILITY TO THE UK

Official Urban Air Mobility ecosystem kick-off in London

21 November 2018

RESERVE A PLACE

London, United Kingdom

Unlocking the 3rd dimension

The Aviary Project Launch Event is bringing together the key stakeholders of aerial mobility systems to accelerate the adoption of eVTOLs, which will alleviate urban problems such as congestion, air pollution, excessive commuting and increase efficiency in various other use-cases such as emergency services.

With professionals from vehicle design, corporations & startups, airspace integration companies, regulators, consultancies, infrastructure, and government agencies attending, the event brings together the greatest minds in this industry to make urban aviation a reality.

Agenda

Round Tables (invite-only)

14:30 - 18:00

EVENT FULL
Round Table A: State-of-the-Art of UAM technologies

The agenda will be focused on the current state of the UAM industry, leveraging the perspectives of all stakeholders with regards to today and the future.

Round Table B: London Ecosystem

A deep dive into London and the UK as a use-case, working towards establishing a consortium and a roadmap to conduct a network design of the city.

Networking Dinner

Food and drinks in a networking setting to formally conclude the discussion from the invite-only part of the event.

Main Event (public)

18:00 - 21:00

Speakers

Expert Panel

Networking Drinks

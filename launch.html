BRINGING AERIAL MOBILITY TO THE UK

Official kick-off of the Urban Air Mobility ecosystem in London.

22 November 2018

RESERVE A PLACE

London, United Kingdom

Unlocking the 3rd dimension

The Aviary Project Launch Event is bringing together the key stakeholders of aerial mobility systems to accelerate the adoption of eVTOLs, which will alleviate urban problems such as congestion, air pollution, excess commuting and increase efficiency in various other use-cases such as emergency services.

With professionals from vehicle design corporations & startups, airspace integration companies, regulators, consultancies, infrastructure, and government agencies attending, the event brings together the greatest minds in this industry to make urban aviation a reality.

Round Table A: State-of-the-Art of UAM technologies

The agenda will be focused on the current state of the UAM industry, leveraging the perspectives of all stakeholders with regards to today and the future. The scope will be based around the following questions:

  • How does each stakeholder fit into UAM and what is the current outlook?
  • What are the obstacles faced by each organization in the UAM ecosystem?
  • How can we create a working collaboration to make the UK a leading player in Urban Air Mobility?

    • Round Table B: London Ecosystem

    A dive deep into London and the UK as a use-case, working towards establishing a consortium and a roadmap to conduct a potential network design of the city.

  • What are the specifics of eVTOL operations in London?
  • What are the requirements from the perspective of each stakeholder?
  • How should the UAM integration strategy and roadmap look like?
